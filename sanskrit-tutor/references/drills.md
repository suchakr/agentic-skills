# Drill Bank

Use these formats for quick practice.

## Drill 1: Lakara choice

Prompt format:
- Provide meaning in English.
- Ask learner to produce one Sanskrit verb form.

Answer format:
- Correct form in Devanagari.
- One-line reason for lakara choice.

## Drill 2: Vibhakti substitution

Prompt format:
- Give base noun + target syntactic role.
- Ask for correct inflected form.

Answer format:
- Correct form.
- Case/number label.

## Drill 3: Sandhi to Padachheda

Prompt format:
- Give one compact phrase with sandhi.
- Ask learner to split into members.

Answer format:
- Correct split.
- One-line justification.

## Drill 4: Composition repair

Prompt format:
- Provide one flawed learner sentence.
- Ask learner to fix.

Answer format:
- Correct sentence.
- Correction list (`old -> new`).
